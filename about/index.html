About JL Solutions

We build systems that let B2B businesses focus on what they do best

JL Solutions exists to help small and mid-size B2B teams, from home services to clinics, caterers, and nonprofits, scale without breaking the office. Most teams are great at the work itself; back-office processes are too often stuck on sticky notes, voicemail, and slow workflows.

Who we are

Practical automation for real operations

We design automated systems that:

  • Capture every lead or request automatically
  • Route work, appointments, or jobs efficiently
  • Trigger approvals, quotes, confirmations, and follow-ups
  • Scale during peak periods without extra chaos

Goal: more clients served, faster service, less admin stress, and owners who can finally sleep.

About us

We are Jess and Brittany, co-founders of JL Solutions.

We built JL Solutions because too many businesses are stuck using disconnected tools, outdated workflows, and systems that create more work instead of less. The result is slower operations, missed opportunities, and frustrating customer experiences.

Jess brings deep experience in full stack development, UX, and business process design, with a background building secure, user-focused solutions across government, healthcare, nonprofit, and private-sector environments.

Brittany brings a thoughtful, people-centered perspective and a strong focus on strategy, operations, and long-term business value. Having known each other since grade school, we built JL Solutions on a real foundation of trust, collaboration, and shared vision.

Together, we focus on practical automation, better user experiences, and systems that help businesses run smoother, serve better, and grow with less friction.

How we are different

Why JL Solutions (not another agency)

  • Operator-led. We are biased toward systems that survive real call volume, seasonality, and staff turnover, not slide decks.
  • B2B and service-first. We specialize in teams where missed intake or slow follow-up means lost revenue: field services, clinics, catering, nonprofits, and similar operations.
  • No mystery stack. You should understand what you are paying for: where data goes, who gets notified, and how to change a rule when the business evolves.
  • Proof over hype. We point to concrete flows (capture, qualify, schedule, report) so you can judge fit before you commit.
