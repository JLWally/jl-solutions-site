üí° Titi's Tips for Success

Don't try to memorize everything

Even experienced developers Google things constantly. Focus on understanding concepts, not memorizing syntax.

Build projects, not just tutorials

Following tutorials is great, but you'll learn 10x faster by building your own projects.

Debugging is a skill

Don't get frustrated when things break. Every bug you fix makes you a better developer.

Ask for help when stuck

Don't waste hours stuck on one problem. Text me, search Stack Overflow, or ask in communities.
