We Build Systems That Let B2B Businesses Focus on What They Do Best

Who We Are

JL Solutions exists to help small-to-mid B2B companies - from home service teams to mental health clinics, caterers, and nonprofits - scale without breaking their office. Most businesses are amazing at what they do, but back-office processes are stuck on sticky notes, voicemail, and slow workflows.

We design automated systems that:

  • Capture every lead or request automatically
  • Route work, appointments, or jobs efficiently
  • Trigger approvals, quotes, confirmations, and follow-ups
  • Scale seamlessly during peak periods or high-demand events

Goal: More clients served, faster service, less admin stress. That's what makes owners sleep better at night.

How we work

We keep the same simple rhythm you’ll see on our homepage: find where things break, build automation that fits how your team actually works, then launch with training and support so it sticks.

Identify the bottlenecks

We map intake, scheduling, and follow-up so we are fixing the real leaks—not layering software on top of chaos.

Build the automation

Practical workflows, forms, routing, and integrations sized to your volume—not a generic “AI transformation” deck.

Launch with confidence

We help you go live, refine, and scale so busy weeks do not mean broken processes.

Why JL Solutions (not another agency)

  • Operator-led. We are biased toward systems that survive real call volume, seasonality, and staff turnover—not slide decks.
  • B2B and service-first. We specialize in teams where missed intake or slow follow-up directly means lost revenue: field services, clinics, catering, nonprofits, and similar operations.
  • No mystery stack. You should understand what you are paying for: where data goes, who gets notified, and how to change a rule when your business evolves.
  • Proof over hype. We point to concrete flows—capture, qualify, schedule, report—so you can judge fit before you commit.

Founder-led, direct work

JL Solutions is led by Jess Walcott. Clients work with people who own the outcome—not a revolving roster of account managers. That keeps decisions fast, implementations grounded in your real constraints, and follow-through consistent from first call to launch.
