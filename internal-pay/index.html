Common questions

When will I get my receipt?

Immediately after successful payment, Stripe emails your receipt to the address you entered.

What happens after I pay a deposit?

We’ll follow up with onboarding details, next steps, and anything needed to begin the project.

What if I entered the wrong amount?

Stop before paying and contact us. If you already paid, email us right away so we can fix it.

Do I need a call before I can pay or try a demo?

No. You can view the live demo, try the document extraction demo, or complete checkout on this page without scheduling a call, as long as you know what you are paying for. If you are not sure yet, contact us or Book a Free Call and we’ll help you choose.
