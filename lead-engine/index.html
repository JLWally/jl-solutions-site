Pipeline: Analyze → Score → Draft → Approve → Send. Gen/Apr/Sent = latest email outreach by status. History = full timeline + send recovery.

Demo email vs reminder date: Demo stage (dropdown) is where this lead is in your custom-demo email workflow. Remind on (column) is only the calendar date for the next nudge—it does not replace stage. Use Email sent or the stage dropdown → “Marked sent (manual)” when they got the demo link. Set reminder sets stage to “Follow-up needed” and a due date (+3 business days UTC). Open outreach opens the email composer. Edit lead updates company, URL, and contact email.
