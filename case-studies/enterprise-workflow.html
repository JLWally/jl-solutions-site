Enterprise Workflow Integration

Connected disparate systems and automated cross-department workflows

50% Faster Processing
$1.8M Cost Reduction
24/7 Automation
"The integration has eliminated bottlenecks and improved our entire operation."
— COO

The Challenge

An enterprise client had multiple systems that didn't talk to each other. Orders, inventory, and customer data lived in silos. Manual handoffs between departments caused delays and errors, and no one had a single view of the full process.

Our Approach

JL Solutions designed and implemented a workflow integration that:

  • Connects CRM, ERP, and internal tools through automated data flows
  • Triggers cross-department workflows based on events (e.g., order placed → inventory check → fulfillment)
  • Runs 24/7 so processes continue outside business hours
  • Provides a unified dashboard for operations visibility

Results

The company achieved 50% faster processing, $1.8M in annual cost reduction, and round-the-clock automation that eliminated bottlenecks. The COO gained real-time visibility into operations for the first time.
