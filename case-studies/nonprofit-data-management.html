Nonprofit Data Management System

Automated intake processes and donor management for a growing nonprofit organization

60% Process Efficiency
3x Capacity Increase
95% Accuracy
"We can now serve more clients with the same team size. It's been transformative."
- Executive Director

The Challenge

A growing nonprofit was overwhelmed by manual intake processes. Staff spent hours screening applicants, entering data into spreadsheets, and chasing down missing information. Donor management was scattered across multiple tools, and reporting for grants was a nightmare.

Our Approach

JL Solutions built a custom intake and data management system that:

  • Guides applicants through eligibility screening with smart forms and wizards
  • Automatically validates and routes applications to the right team
  • Centralizes donor data and triggers thank-you and follow-up communications
  • Generates reports for funders with one click

Results

The organization achieved 60% improvement in process efficiency, tripled the number of clients they could serve with the same staff, and reached 95% data accuracy. Grant reporting that used to take days now takes hours.
